What We Do

Services We Offer

Expert diagnostics and repair for every type of gas fireplace issue.

🔍

Diagnostics & Repair

Full inspection of igniters, thermocouples, gas valves, pilot lights, and controls.

🛡️

Annual Tune-Up

Seasonal maintenance to ensure safe operation, clean burners, and maximize efficiency.

🔥

Pilot Light Issues

Fix pilots that won't stay lit, intermittent ignition problems, or repeated outages.

🪟

Glass & Gasket

Replace cracked or cloudy fireplace glass panels and worn door gaskets.

Thermostat & Remote

Repair or replace wall thermostats, remote receivers, and control systems.

Parts Replacement

OEM and compatible parts for all major brands with upfront quotes.
